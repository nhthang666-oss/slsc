About Us

Saigon Supply Chain Logistics Co., Ltd. was established with a clear mission: to provide reliable, end-to-end logistics solutions that connect Vietnam with the global market. We combine local expertise, a professional operations team, and strategic international partners to deliver freight forwarding, customs clearance, warehousing and distribution services that meet modern supply-chain requirements.

Our core values: Integrity, Reliability, Customer Focus. We continuously invest in people and systems so our customers enjoy transparent tracking, timely delivery and competitive rates.

  • Experienced freight management across sea, air and road
  • Customs & compliance support to minimize delays
  • Flexible warehousing and value-added services

Our Services

Sea Freight

Full Container Load (FCL), Less than Container Load (LCL), break-bulk and project cargo handling. We partner with major carriers to provide reliable schedules and competitive rates.

Air Freight

Priority and economy air solutions, including charter options and door-to-door express services for urgent shipments.

Customs Brokerage & Compliance

Experienced customs brokerage team to handle documentation, classification and regulatory requirements, ensuring faster clearance and reduced compliance risk.

Warehousing & Distribution

Secure storage, inventory management, pick-and-pack, labelling and cross-docking with flexible short-term and long-term solutions.

Supply Chain Solutions

End-to-end supply chain planning, procurement support, last-mile delivery and tailor-made logistics programs to fit different industries.

Project & Specialized Cargo

Handling oversized, heavy-lift and complex project shipments with coordinated planning, equipment and on-site support.

Our Offices & Network

Headquartered in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, Saigon Supply Chain operates through a network of branch offices and trusted partners across Asia and around the world. This network enables smooth door-to-door services and local on-ground support at origin and destination.

Ho Chi Minh City (HQ)

Corporate headquarters and main operations hub. Address: 123 Nguyen Van Linh, District 7.

Hanoi

Northern Vietnam office handling inbound/outbound operations and customs for the North.

Hai Phong

Port-side presence to support sea freight and export operations in the North.

Regional Partners

Strong partner network in Thailand, Singapore, China, Korea and Japan to provide regional coverage.

If you need local assistance in another country, please contact us and we will connect you with our nearest partner or agent.
